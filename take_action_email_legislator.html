Tell your legislators to pass the Healthy and Safe Students Act

The Healthy and Safe Students Act (S6901A / A7496A) has been introduced in the New York State Legislature every session since 2017. It has broad co-sponsorship and no organized opposition — yet it has never received a floor vote. Passing this bill with an explicit extension to detention, alternative education, and probation settings is the foundational statutory fix that every other reform depends on.
